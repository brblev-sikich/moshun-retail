SmarteHinge™ Modular Platform

A modular hinge system for limitless possibilities

The SmarteHinge™ modular platform starts with the base hinge component. Slots are positioned on the top and bottom of the hinge in which modules can be inserted to influence the door’s closing behavior. From soft-closing to slam control, Moshun’s SmarteHinge™ modules offer you a pick-and-choose assortment to meet your needs.

It is possible on a three hinge door to unleash the functionality of up to six modules, all with one consistent and discrete design.
