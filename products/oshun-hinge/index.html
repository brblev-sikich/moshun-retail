Elegant marine door control

  • Control abrupt door movement.
  • Manage slamming through the full range of door travel.

  • Integrated backstop for set and forget door position – eliminate deck and wall mounted stops.
  • Connect to multi-function displays (MFD) to stay informed on door position through monitoring and alerts.
  • polished or satin 316 stainless steel.
  • Integrated yacht brand logo.
  • Available in many finishes and sizes to fit existing doors.
