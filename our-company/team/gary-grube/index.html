Gary Grube

Gary W. Grube

Intellectual Property

About Gary

Gary, a registered U.S. Patent Agent, is founder and CEO of the Caerus Institute, an intellectual property based private equity endeavor with a proven track record of creating IP value, and a prolific inventor with over 427 issued U.S. patents!

Prior to the Caerus Institute, Gary served in various management and research and development roles at Motorola, including as Chief Technical Officer and was the first Motorola Senior Fellow to be named. Gary has served on many industry, university, advisory, and non-profit boards, provided expert testimony in front of the U.S. Congress on public safety matters, and provided his expertise to patent litigation cases.

Gary enjoys leveraging broad business insights and technical acumen to zero in on unmet customer needs with differentiated solutions. He holds an MBA from Northwestern University, a MS in Electrical Engineering from the Illinois Institute of Technology, and an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois.

In his spare time Gary enjoys participating in high performance driving track events in his Dodge Viper. His passion is his foundation and vegetable farm in Hawaii providing fresh produce to restaurants, and helping alleviate food insecurity by donating 50 tons of food every year.
