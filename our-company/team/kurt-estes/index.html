About Kurt

Kurt is a Managing Director at Sikich Corporate Finance where he leads the technology M&A vertical as well as all corporate development initiatives for Sikich as a whole. He brings 20 years of transactional experience advising on sales and acquisitions as well as investing in and creating businesses around innovative technologies. He has conducted transactions covering all stages of a companyâ€™s life, from acquiring seed investments through the facilitation of liquidity events. He has also negotiated many deals including asset buyouts, recapitalizations, spinouts, strategic partnerships, licensing agreements, and various debt structures.

With an MBA from Northwestern and Mechanical Engineering degrees from Purdue University, Kurt brings a multitude of talents to Moshun, as an engineer, author, inventor, and entrepreneur.

Kurt is registered with the Finacial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) as a Series 24 General Securities Principal, a Series 7 General Securities Representative, a Series 79 Investment Banking Representative, and a Series 63 Uniform Securities Agent.
